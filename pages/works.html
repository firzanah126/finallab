How does it work

With 3 easy steps

  1. Search - our extensive list of quality babysitters and nannies.
  2. Post a job - to attract the perfect carers to you.
  3. Select - interview and choose the best babysitter or nanny for your family.

Why Find A Babysitter?

Quality

As parents ourselves we genuinely care about helping you get the best quality babysitters and nannies for your family.

Trusted

For over 15 years Find A Babysitter has helped thousands of parents find trusted carers. We are focused on ensuring you meet trusted carers, with our systems and ratings.

Proven Results

You get results. Weve helped over 70,000 parents around Australia find FAB babysitters and nannies. See our testimonials sharing the success parents have had finding the perfect carer on Find A Babysitter.

Experienced

We were the very first site in Australia to build this concept, so we bring all of our knowledge, experience and passion to ensure Find A Babysitter is the best site for you.

Award-winning

Find A Babysitter has won several awards, including the State and National Telstra Business Award for Microbusiness (2008), recognising our focus on quality and results.

Friendly Customer Service

FAB gives you friendly, kind and responsive customer service.

Choice & Freedom

You choose the best match for your family. We cut out the middleman, giving you complete choice and freedom.

Affordability

We dont process payments, or charge any fees or overheads. You pay the sitter directly, saving you money.

Authenticity

Find A Babysitter is run by Delia Timms and Jeff Bonnes (see About Us for more details). We genuinely care about you, your family and our fabulous babysitters. This brings a personal and professional focus, to ensure the site gives you great results.
