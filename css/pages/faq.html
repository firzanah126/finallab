Parent FAQs

What is Find A Babysitter?

Find A Babysitter is Australia's most trusted online childcare job site, helping you locate the best nannies, after-school carers and babysitters.
Find A Babysitter is not an agency. We simply put you in touch with a range of great babysitters and nannies, then you interview and select the best one for you.
With Find A Babysitter you'll have the confidence and calm that comes with having the perfect carer for your children. So you can manage the busy juggling act of children, work, social and family life.
You get freedom, choice, flexibility and affordability.
Freedom to connect with babysitters directly, as theres no 'middle-man controlling the process.

Why Find A Babysitter?

  • Quality - As parents ourselves we genuinely care about helping you get the best babysitters and nannies for your family.
  • Trusted - For over 15 years Find A Babysitter has helped thousands of parents find trusted carers.
  • Experienced - We were the very first site in Australia to build this concept, so we bring all of our knowledge, experience and passion to ensure Find A Babysitter is the best site.
  • Award-winning - Find A Babysitter is a National Telstra Business Award winner.
  • Customer service - FAB gives you friendly, kind and responsive customer service.

How does it work?

  1. Search & send emails: Search in your postcode and send out 8-10 messages to FAB carers who meet your criteria.
  2. Post a job: Make use of the Job Posting to draw suitable applicants to you.
  3. Screen & select: Conduct phone screenings, interview and reference check to select the right carer for your family. Then you are all set, the babysitter is yours.
